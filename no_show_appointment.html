Conclusions¶

in the end we can say from information that we gathered:

  • people who have big differance between ScheduledDay and AppointmentDay (diff) tend to miss appointments more than people who have less (diff), also sending sms help making them show
  • the range of ages from 10 - 30 have higher chance to miss than other ages
  • locations of hospital don't make that big differance
